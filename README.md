### Changelog - Office Clicker v0.9.2

#### 🔧 Fixes & Mathematical Balancing
* **Balanced Economy Curve:** Standardized cost scaling constants (Click Upgrades at 1.15x, Automation at 1.12x) to smooth out progression and prevent severe cost walls.
* **PowerPoint Pedant Calibration:** Balanced the early-game random roll calculation down to a sustainable +1 to +4 per click.
* **Accelerated Abbreviation Engine:** Re-indexed numeric formatting thresholds to transition directly into short-scale notations at every 100 Million interval (e.g., 100M scales cleanly to 1.00 Billion).
* **Crash Resolution:** Patched a critical global news-marquee array reference typo (`HEADREVO_BANKS`) that caused the game to crash at 1 Billion lifetime emails.

#### 🛠️ New Features & UI Tooling
* **Departmental Tooling Upgrades:** Added a full suite of double-tiered, department-specific automation milestones (+20% and +50% production modifiers) that unlock dynamically based on staffing counts.
* **Layout Optimization:** Repositioned the Corporate Performance Cabinet firmly to the bottom of the workstation console to optimize screen space.
* **Collapsible Analytics:** Segmented Personal Development, Departmental Assets, and Workforce Records inside the performance cabinet into independently collapsible, clean sub-panels.

#### ✨ Visual Juice & Polish
* **Dynamic Cursor Particles:** Integrated a performance-optimized floating text stream spawning active click counts exactly under the mouse cursor.
* **Screen Shake Physics:** Tied a hardware-accelerated screen shake to the metrics panel whenever a PowerPoint Pedant upgrade hits a "Perfect Alignment" critical roll.
* **Enhanced Buff Badges:** Added a visual progress tracking bar to active Coffee Rush banners that flashes with a warning animation when duration drops to $\le 4$ seconds.
* **UI Precision:** Formatted the Global Investment Power metric to cleanly support localized thousands-comma dividers.
